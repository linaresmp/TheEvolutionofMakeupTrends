THE

EVOLUTION

OF MAKEUP TRENDS!

The Evolution of Makeup Trends is a set of three p5.js interactive canvases showcasing the change in makeup trends from the 1980’s to the 2010’s. Each canvas focuses on one specific makeup evolution and the celebrities that brought it to life.

Click the canvas and then press the up arrow to see how eyebrow trends have changed over the years

BROW ARE YOU DOING?

The bigger the better! That's what Brooke Shields used to say about her brows in the 1980's. Drew Barrymore did not feel the same in the 1990's and sported the razor thin brow trend. Gigi Hadid has become a big fan of the outlined thick eyebrow that has become all the rave in recent years.

EYES AND SHINE

Eyeshadows are the perfect canvas to showcase the biggest color trends at the time. Whitney Houston flaunted the blue, pink and purple shadows on her eyes. Britney Spears was the face of glitter eyeshadow in the 1990's and Kim Kardashian reinvented nude eyeshadow in the late 2010's.

Click the canvas and start clicking through the eye palettes to view the difference in eyeshadow trends.

Hover your cursor over the image and watch how lip colors have changed over the years

LIP TIPS

A look isn't complete until the lipstick is on! In the 80's lipsticks were bright and bold and done best by Madonna. The 1990's were all about the liner and the 2010's brought along the now iconic Kylie Jenner matte lipstick.
